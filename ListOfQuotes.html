page 249: “Later, when he looked back, the yellow cabin was gone. But even then, in his mind’s eye, he could see a man and a woman lying quietly on a porch in the dense night fog…{This man and woman being John and Kathy}... and soon the fog was all around them and inside them and they were swallowed up and gone.” Magicians often use smoke (fog works too) to disappear.
